Last updated: 19 April 2026

These Terms govern your use of Sanad-AI ("the service"), an automated assistant that connects citizens and residents of the Sultanate of Oman to licensed Sanad offices via WhatsApp and web.

1 · Who we are

Sanad-AI is an independent service operator that integrates with licensed Sanad offices. We are not the Government of Oman, nor affiliated with the official government Sanad portal. The actual government service (e.g. licence issuance) is performed by the Sanad office and the relevant government entity.

2 · Eligibility

  • You must be 18+, or a legal guardian acting for a minor.
  • You must be a citizen or legal resident of Oman, or authorised to transact on behalf of one.
  • You must provide accurate, truthful information. Submitting false documents is a criminal offense under Omani law.

3 · What the service does

Sanad-AI receives your request in natural language, collects the documents the Sanad office requires, routes the request to a licensed officer who completes the government-side transaction on your behalf, and relays the result back to you. We do not ourselves issue any government document.

4 · Fees

  • Government fees are set by the relevant entity and passed through unchanged.
  • The Sanad office may charge its own service fee as permitted by Omani regulation.
  • Sanad-AI may charge a technology fee, displayed clearly before you confirm submission.
  • All fees are shown in Omani Rial (OMR) before confirmation.

5 · Your obligations

  • Do not attempt to use the service for fraudulent, impersonation, money-laundering, or otherwise illegal purposes.
  • Do not abuse, harass, or threaten officers or operations staff.
  • Do not attempt to reverse-engineer, scrape, or disrupt the service.
  • You are responsible for keeping your WhatsApp number secure; any message from your number is treated as originating from you.

6 · OTP and shared credentials

Some government services require a one-time password (OTP) delivered to your phone. When an officer needs it, the assistant will prompt you explicitly. Sanad-AI will never ask for your permanent password, bank PIN, or credit-card CVV.

7 · Service availability

We aim for 24/7 availability, but the service depends on upstream systems (the government portal, Meta / WhatsApp, our hosting provider). We do not guarantee uninterrupted operation and are not liable for delays or failures caused by those upstream systems.

8 · AI limitations

For legally binding information, refer to the official government source or consult the assigned Sanad officer. We are not liable for financial loss resulting from reliance on AI-generated replies without officer confirmation.

9 · Cancellation and refunds

  • You may cancel a request before it is accepted by an officer at no charge.
  • After an officer has started work, partial fees may be non-refundable depending on the Sanad office's own policy, displayed at confirmation.
  • If the government entity rejects the request for reasons outside your control (e.g. system outage), the technology fee is refundable.

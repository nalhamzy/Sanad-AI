State definitions
Citizen submits
Bot/form intake — fees fixed at catalog snapshot (5 + 15 = 20 OMR default).
pending_review
In pool. Eligible online offices in the visibility tier compete via FCFS atomic UPDATE.
under_review
Office holds the claim. 15-min clock for one of three actions.
awaiting_documents
Office requested missing/clearer docs. Same office retains claim, citizen has 24h.
awaiting_payment
Payment link sent. Citizen has 15min to pay 20 OMR.
in_progress
Citizen paid. Office has 45min for gov-portal work.
on_hold
Office paused (portal down / OTP / etc.). 4h max; clock resumes from snapshot.
completed ✓
Done. Anonymous receipt to citizen.
rejected_by_office ✗
Hard reject (fraud / wrong service / unfixable). Citizen may re-apply.
cancelled_by_citizen ✗
Pre-pay cancel. Free, no refund flow.
refund_pending
Post-pay cancel. Office decides; admin can override in 7d.
refunded ✓
Refund processed (full or partial).
Three locked rules
  1. Fixed pricing — catalog: office_fee=5 + government_fee=15 default. No bidding.
  2. Citizen sees status, not options — no office picker, no offer comparison, no marketplace UI on citizen side.
  3. Citizen anonymity — receipts, status updates, chat thread, SLA transfers all use platform voice. Office name never on citizen surface.
Discipline (no credits in MVP)
  • Capacity caps: Starter 3 / Pro 12 / Enterprise ∞. At cap → invisible to marketplace.
  • Reputation ranking: rating × 4 + completion_rate × 2 − abandonment_rate × 4 − work_breaches × 1.5 + …
  • Auto-suspension: 3 review breaches OR 1 work breach / 7d → 24-h pause. 5+ → hard suspend, manual review.
  • credit_ledger + credits_remaining columns kept dormant for re-introduction without migration.
